// A Word On Safety

Wrap your blades, please.

A folded kitchen towel, piece of cardboard, or bubble wrap taped around each blade is all it takes. Empty cardboard tubes work great for longer knives. This keeps your fingers, my fingers, and anyone's fingers in between, perfectly attached — the way they ought to be.
